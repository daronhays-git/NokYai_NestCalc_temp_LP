```
### [YYYY-MM-DD] — TITLE
- **Source:** (github-issue | email | manual | in-app)

- **Severity:** (unscored | minor | major | critical | suggestion)
- **Status:** (new | triaged | scheduled | resolved)
- **GitHub Issue:** #N or n/a
- **Description:** One-paragraph summary of the feedback.
```
